CH410/510 Scientific Computing¶

Overview¶

  • The class will meet Mon and Wed at 9 am 1 hr, and then Fri for 2 hr.

  • In general, Mon and Wed will be instruction days where we cover new programming material. Fri will be an open “lab” where you will work on exercises as groups in class.

  • Because of COVID-19, all class time and office hours will be done via zoom. The class will meet remotely at the times above. Details are here.
